$$
\text{ECR}(m) = \frac{1}{|F|} \sum_{f \in F} \min \left(100, \frac{df_m}{df^\star} \right)
$$
where $F$ is the set of benchmark functions, $df_m$ is the distance between the global minimum of $f$ and the approximation found by the method $m$, and $df^\star$ is the smallest distance among all the methods.
